Site Plan: Volcano High School Portal

Site Name

The name of the site is the Volcano High School Portal.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to serve as the official online portal for Volcano High School, providing prospective and current students and their families with comprehensive information on academics, the application process, campus life, and upcoming events.

Scenarios

  • Prospective Student/Parent: Where can I find the application form and what are the different tuition plans (Day Scholar, Boarding, International)?
  • Current Student/Parent: What are the upcoming school events and what is the current campus weather?
  • Any Visitor: What classes and activities are offered, and how can I see a virtual tour of the campus?

Color Schema

The color scheme for the site conveys trust, stability, and intelligence, suitable for an educational institution.

Category / Where to be used Color (Hex)
Primary Color #0056B3
Secondary Color #FFC107
Background Color #F4F4F9
Text Color #212529

Typography

The typography for the site will use the following fonts for a balance of professionalism and readability:

  • Headings: Montserrat
  • Body Text: Open Sans
